Walk. Root. Rise. A spiritual nature immersion experience where you reclaim your power and sovereignty.

Rooted & Rising gently invites you to reclaim what was always yours: your loving authority, your groundedness, and your sovereign self.

We gather at Mt. Madonna County Park for a morning walk through the beauty of nature, followed by a short, intimate teaching drawn from current books and spiritual wisdom.

Our first session explores the teachings of David Hawkins from Power vs. Force, the difference between dominating force and loving aligned power, and how you can step fully into your own without overpowering anyone around you.

What to expect:

  • A gentle hike to a peaceful forest clearing
  • A 15-20 minute teaching that points you toward your own wisdom
  • A solo nature exercise to embody the teaching in a felt way
  • A buddy exercise where you share your experience and speak your truth out loud
  • A grounded, intimate experience for stepping into your own power

What to bring:

  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • A mat or small tarp to sit on
  • Plenty of water
  • Snacks if you need them
  • An open heart
