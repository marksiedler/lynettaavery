Freedom Through Frequency is a sound and energy experience co-facilitated by Lynetta Ann Avery and Ember Paige. Together they weave Reiki, sound, and vibrational tools into a grounded, heart-centered space that supports deep alignment and restoration.

This session honors the arc from the Full Moon in Sagittarius through the New Moon in Gemini — moving through the themes of release, curiosity, new beginnings, and the harmony that comes when we integrate what we have learned and open to what is next.

Lie back, breathe deeply, and allow the healing frequencies of crystal singing bowls, Tibetan bowls, chimes, and tuning forks to wash over your body and quiet your mind. Reiki energy is woven throughout. For those who give consent, Lynetta offers gentle hands-on Reiki during the bath.

Common Questions

What to Know Before You Come

Where do I park?

Plenty of parking is available in the back of the building. SoulSanc is easy to find, right off the 280 at Saratoga Ave in San Jose.

Is the sound bath loud?

Not at all. The sound is very gentle and intentional. Crystal singing bowls, Tibetan bowls, and tuning forks are played softly to support deep rest and nervous system relaxation. Many people find it easier to relax here than anywhere else.

I have never done a sound bath before. Is this right for me?

Absolutely. No experience is needed and there is nothing you have to do except lie down and receive. Everything is set up for your comfort. You are welcome exactly as you are.

What is the refund policy?

Because space is limited to 10 guests, we kindly ask that you cancel at least 48 hours before the event to receive a full refund. Cancellations within 48 hours are non-refundable, though your payment may be applied to a future event.
