The Approach

You're Not Looking for Someone to Fix You.

You've already tried that. You want to learn how to heal yourself. You want to be proactive. You want to understand what your body's been trying to say all along, and then actually live that wisdom in your life every single day.

In a session with me, we start with your breath. I guide you to notice where you feel pain or tension in your body. Then we pause. We listen. Your body has been trying to tell you something, and most of us have never stopped long enough to hear it.

Together we use sound, Reiki, and guided meditation to help you go deeper into that listening. You start to understand what your body needs. And then you take that wisdom home with you.

After your session, I give you a guided meditation that's custom made for what we worked on together. You listen to it every day. You practice. You integrate. That's where the real healing happens.

— Lynetta Avery
