Group Offering

Group Sound Baths

A group sound bath is a vibrational immersion. Together we drop into a deep delta state, the slowest, most restorative brainwave frequency, where the body finally exhales and the nervous system remembers how to rest.

This is a collective experience. As each person deepens into relaxation, everyone in the room relaxes a little deeper. We begin to entrain with one another. The dissonance softens. Resonance builds. And something remarkable happens: we arrive at a state of coherence together.

The walls dissolve. The ceiling opens. We find ourselves in the vast universe, expanding together.

Reiki energy is woven throughout the session. For those who give consent, Lynetta offers gentle hands-on Reiki during the bath, bringing the work even more intimately into the body.

  • Guided breathwork to open and settle
  • Crystal singing bowls, Tibetan bowls, and chimes
  • Tuning forks, played and applied on the body
  • Reiki energy woven throughout
  • Gentle hands-on Reiki for consenting participants

Private Offering

Private Sound Bath & Healing Sessions

A private session is a space of deep, unhurried listening. We begin with a conversation so I can understand what you are carrying. From there we weave together sound healing, tuning forks applied directly on the body, and Reiki to meet you exactly where you are.

We work as a team. I hold the space, the sound, and the energy. You bring your willingness to feel into what is there, to sit with it, and to receive whatever wisdom the body offers. What we do together is take that wisdom and begin to weave it back into your life.

Private sessions are a good fit for those moving through grief, stress, trauma, or life transitions, and for anyone who is ready to stop living in reaction and start coming home to themselves.

  • Intake conversation to set intention
  • Crystal singing bowls and Tibetan bowls
  • Tuning forks applied directly on the body
  • Reiki energy healing
  • Closing integration and reflection
