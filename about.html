My Story

I Know What It Feels Like to Live in Dissonance.

Holy Fire® III Karuna Reiki® Sound Bath Guide Tuning Forks Practitioner Family Constellations 25+ Years

I am Lynetta Ann Avery. I come from a family where everyone lived in separate rooms. We didn't know how to relate to each other. There was dissonance. I felt it my whole life. I didn't have the language for it then, but I can see now what happens when we live separate, feeling all alone.

"Sound, energy, and vibration are beyond separation. They do what words cannot."

They move through the walls we build between ourselves and the people we love, between our minds and our bodies, between who we are and who we were always meant to be.

My journey into the healing arts began in 2001 with a Massage and Reiki practice. What started as hands-on bodywork gradually opened into something deeper. Somatic healing. Vibrational medicine. The study of what happens in the body when we finally get still enough to feel what has always been there.

Over the years my path wove through Family Constellations, sound healing, tuning fork therapy, and the transformational work I share through my platform Why This Keeps Happening — dedicated to helping people understand and move through the patterns that keep them living in separation from themselves and each other.

I am trained in the William Lee Rand lineage of Holy Fire® III Karuna Reiki® and hold the Master Teacher level, offering placements (attunements) at all three levels to those who are ready to carry this work forward.

What I know for certain is this: we are not meant to live in dissonance. We are wired for harmony, for resonance, for connection. That is what we come back to — together — in every session.

Every experience I offer is here to return you to your natural state of coherence, in your heart, your mind, your body, and your spirit.
