My Story

I Know What it Feels Like to Live in Pain.

Holy Fire® III Karuna Reiki® Sound Bath Guide Tuning Forks Practitioner Family Constellations 25+ Years

I am Lynetta Ann Avery.

I grew up in a house where everyone lived in separate rooms. We were shut down. I felt completely alone. I couldn't express myself or my needs. When I spoke up, I got in trouble. So I learned to shove my feelings way down deep.

The only way I survived was to leave my body.

My whole life has been the journey back.

In 2001 my own body broke down and I had no choice but to listen. That's when I found the healing arts. Not as a career. As a way home. What started as massage and Reiki opened into something deeper. Somatic healing. Vibrational medicine. Family Constellations. Sound healing. Tuning fork therapy. Each one another step back into myself.

Everything I offer comes from that path.

People come to me the way they go to a mechanic or a doctor. Fix it. Take the pain away.

That's not what I do.

I want to work myself out of a job. I want you to have such mastery with your own body that you don't need me anymore. Once you learn this, you can apply it to any pain, any pattern, anything that shows up in your body. It's not hard to learn. It just takes your commitment.

You come to me when you're ready to be fully embodied and full of life.

I am a Holy Fire® III Karuna Reiki® Master Teacher, Sound Bath Guide, and Tuning Forks Practitioner. I share this work through my platform Why This Keeps Happening, dedicated to helping people move through the patterns that keep them living separate from themselves and each other.

That separation is what I grew up in. I know exactly what it costs.

And I know the way back.
