Our Story

A Place to Finally Put It Down

We've been together fourteen years, married thirteen, as of June 2nd, 2026.

When we found each other, we were both carrying a childhood that had taught us fear instead of safety. We didn't know it yet, but we were about to give each other something neither of us had ever fully had: a place to finally put it down. He became my sanctuary. I became his. Slowly, in that safety, we each began to heal.

We found each other while each of us was leading people into nature, coaching others toward healing and reconnection with the wild world. We've lived full time in our RV for thirteen years, immersed in nature, because it's where we do our own healing best.

Lynetta's Path

For thirteen years, Lynetta worked as a massage therapist in the heart of Silicon Valley. Underneath her hands, she could feel it: bodies that had become little more than a shelf for the head they carried around all day. She'd guide people gently down into their belly, into their feet, and place warm stones along their spine, bringing a small piece of the earth back into the room. That warmth was often the first thing that brought someone back into their body in years.

Mark's Path

Mark has lived in both worlds too. Twenty-five years in IT support taught him exactly what it costs to live entirely in your head. Long hikes under towering trees taught him what it feels like to come home to your body again. He is most alive under the trees, and so is she.

Our Shared Practice

In the last five years, we've developed a practice of toning together: sounding out whatever we're feeling in our bodies until the tones settle into harmony, and something that was stuck moves. That's the practice we bring into every Circle of Loving Kindness — not performance, not perfection, just sound as a way through.
